Our Story

A fearless yellow jacket landed on the brim of Eric’s lemonade and whiskey-filled mason jar. Christian, an unfamiliar party guest, approached him and photographed the pesky encounter. With the help of this winged pollinator, the two became lovers, and eventually creative business partners. How romantic/gross.
